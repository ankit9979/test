about us

Golf South Africa offers a diverse portfolio of golf tours to one of the world's most memorable destinations – Southern Africa. Our experienced team arranges every detail of personalized itineraries for groups, couples and single golfers. We combine play on the regions leading golf courses with beach relaxation, gourmet food & wine, sightseeing, culture and of course safari. For 10 years we have been creating bespoke golf vacation packages for clients from around the world.
